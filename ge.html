Intro

Hello everyone, here I will be discussing my build log of the mini CNC mill from fabricated from fablab.This project was done under the suprivion of Jaseel and Rashid

DAY 1

Today we discussed what kind of CNC we should make. There were many choices out there. We thought of making a CNC with mostly 3D printed and laser cut parts. So it can be easily fabricated again from Fab itself if any of its parts get damaged due to wear and tear.Since I was short of time I didnt start the build from s. I reffered to some open sourse designs from Thingiverse and finally found a CNC mill with 80% parts which can be fabricated from fab itself.Shown the design to Rashid and he was he ok with design.Since the 3D printer was busy with printing someother project parts I couldnt start the print today

main

DAY 2

Today I started the addictive manufacturing process, there was three 3D printer at Fablab, Dimension, Ultimaker and Aeqon 400.Ultimaker was ideal printer for parts. For slcing I used Cura by Ultimaker. printed all the parts with a infill of 25% and 0.2 Layer height
